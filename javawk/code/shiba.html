Shiba Inu

Know the Breed

The Shiba Inu is a cute, proud, and stubborn breed of dog. They are a hunting dog originating in Japan and are currently the number one most beloved dog breed there. Although the Shiba Inu is prized for its beautiful foxlike appearance and thick orange to tan coat, they come in other colors such as black, cream and sesame. For additional impromation please check the the Shiba Inu American Kennel Club website.

Shiba Inu Attributes

  • Height from 14.5-16.5 inches (male) and 13.5-15.5 inches (female)
  • Weight from 23 pounds (male) and 17 pounds (female)
  • Lifespan from 13-16 years

Where to get your Shiba Inu

  1. Shiba Inus can be purchase from a responsible breeder. Make sure to always meet the parents of your new puppy before purchase.
  2. Shiba Inus can be found in animal shelters or pounds.

Where NOT to get your Shiba Inu

  1. You should NOT purchase a Shiba Inu from a pet store or puppy mill.
  2. Be careful what online sites you buy your puppy from; they may also be from a puppy mill.
